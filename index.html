Our Charity Partners

In alignment with our mission of positive social impact, 60% of all proceeds directly support local organizations making a difference in our community.

Tashkent Children's Educational Foundation

Providing educational resources and learning opportunities for underprivileged children in our city. Their programs focus on literacy, STEM education, and creative arts - perfectly aligned with our educational mission.

Uzbekistan Community Food Network

Addressing food insecurity across Uzbekistan by establishing sustainable food distribution centers and educational programs about nutrition and healthy living.
